Glazed Donuts Holes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 3 to 5 tablespoons frozen grape, cherry-pomegranate or cranberry juice concentrate, thawed
  • 12 doughnut holes

Steps

  1. Whisk together sugar and enough juice concentrate to achieve a thick glaze. Dip doughnut holes in glaze; transfer to waxed paper. Let stand until set.
